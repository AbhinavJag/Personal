Real Purchasing Index Calculation
Nominal earnings are deflated utilizing the BLS CPI-U time chains. Real calculation formula: Nominal Wage × (CPI₂₀₀₀ / CPIₜ), mapping everything directly to 2000 index bases.
Economic Security Composite Metric (ESI)
